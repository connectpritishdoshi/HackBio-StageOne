**Exploring Genomics of Drug Sensitivity in Cancer (GDSC)

#Overview:

In this project, you will explore a real-world dataset on drug sensitivity in cancer, focusing on how genomic features influence response to treatment. Please note that the goal is not to arrive at a single correct answer, but to investigate patterns in the data, ask meaningful and contextual biological questions, demonstrate data various data handling and cleaning techniques and of course, communicate results clearly.

Try as much as possible to be innovative, creative and use clear graphics to showcase your results clearly.

#Team Collaboration Requirement
You are expected to:

a) Work collaboratively in teams to discuss approaches, interpretations, and findings
b) Share ideas and divide tasks where appropriate
c) Strong collaboration will improve the quality of your insights.

#Ideas for exploring the dataset (do not limit yourself)

*Understand the dataset
a) What are the key variables in the dataset
b) What does each column represent (see metadata description)
c) Are there missing values or inconsistencies

*Drug Sensitivity patterns
a) Which drugs appear to be the most effective
b) Which drug show the least effectiveness
c) Are there drugs with highly variable responses across cell lines

*Cancer cell line analysis
a) Do certain cancer types / cell lines respond better to specific drugs
b) What patterns exist in the drug response across cell lines

*Genomic, transcriptomic and epigenomic influence on drug response
a) Are mutations (CNA), gene expression or methylation level influencing drug sensitivity at all?

*Supporting data visualization
a) Distribution plots of drug sensitivity
b) Boxplots comparing drugs or cell lines
c) Scatter plots showing relationships
d) Correlations
